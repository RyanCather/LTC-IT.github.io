Welcome to the Department of Communication & The Arts

About Us

We advise the Australian Government about the communication and arts sectors and deliver programs so Australians can enjoy the benefits of modern communication and cultural expirences. We focus on six (6) major areas. Those are:

  • Internet
    Oversee the rollout of the NBN, Govern competition in broadban and ensure that net-nutrality exists, govern the internet and ensure it meats Australia's standards, consumer safeguards, reform telecommunication to allow for consumers to have better access to broadband networks.
  • Television
    People everywhere watch TV content through their television, computer or mobile device both in real time and in catch up. We adivse the Australian Government about the TV industry and its regulation and services.
  • Phone
    The phone is one of the main ways people stay in touch with friends, family and colleagues. We advise the Australian Government about how all Australian can have reasonable access to a standard and reliable phone service. We ensure 000 and other emergency service numbers work as well as ensuring Australian's have privacy.
  • Radio
    Australians listen to the radio to be entertained, informed and to keep up with developments in emergencies. We advise the Australian Government on how to ensure all Austrlians can access radio. We ensure that commercial radio follows the guidelines amoung many other things.
  • Post
    Australia's postal market is open for most services but only Australia Post delivers letters. We adivse the Austrlian Government about the postal industry and develop policy and draft legislation.
  • Spectrum
    Spectrum refers to the electromagnetic spectrum - the resource that allows us to send wireless signals. Most electronic devices we use today, including smartphones, satellites, tablets, televisions, readio and radars rely on spectrum frequiencies to carry information. We advise the Australian Government about how this scarce resource can be managed effectively and efficiently.
